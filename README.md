# Amazon Product Copier <a href="#-changelog"><img align="right" src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Version-1.0-white.svg" alt="Version: 1.0"></a>&nbsp;<a href="https://github.com/TimMacy/AmazonProductCopier/blob/main/LICENSE"><img align="right" src="https://img.shields.io/badge/License-AGPL--3.0-blue.svg" alt="GNU Affero General Public License v3.0"></a><a href="#"><img align="right" src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Maintained-brightgreen.svg" alt="Amazon Product Copier Status: Maintained"></a>

This userscript adds a copy button to the bottom left of Amazon product pages. With a single click, it captures product information like price, descriptions, details, review scores, including the AI-generated text of what customers said, and more into the clipboard. This streamlines the process of analyzing and comparing items using a preferred AI language model.

#### üîí Privacy Policy
Amazon Product Copier operates completely client-side with no external dependencies and is limited to `https://www.amazon.*/*`.

## üöÄ Installation
1. **Install or open a userscript manager**.
   A userscript manager is required, such as [Userscripts for Safari](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/userscripts/id1463298887), [Tampermonkey](https://www.tampermonkey.net/) (available for [Chrome](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/tampermonkey/dhdgffkkebhmkfjojejmpbldmpobfkfo), [Microsoft Edge](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/tampermonkey/iikmkjmpaadaobahmlepeloendndfphd), [Safari](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/tampermonkey/id1482490089), [Opera Next](https://addons.opera.com/en/extensions/details/tampermonkey-beta/), and [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/tampermonkey/)), or [Violentmonkey](https://violentmonkey.github.io) (available for [Chrome](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/violentmonkey/jinjaccalgkegednnccohejagnlnfdag), [Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/violentmonkey/), and [Microsoft Edge](https://microsoftedge.microsoft.com/addons/detail/violentmonkey/eeagobfjdenkkddmbclomhiblgggliao)).
2. **Install** [AmazonProductCopier.js](https://github.com/TimMacy/AmazonProductCopier/blob/main/AmazonProductCopier.js).
